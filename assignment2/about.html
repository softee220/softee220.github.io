Our Story

KIM Record Store has been serving music lovers for over a decade, providing a unique collection of vinyl records from various genres and artists. Our goal is to keep the essence of music alive through high-quality records and a great shopping experience.

At KIM Record Store, we believe music is more than just sound—it's a timeless experience. Since our founding in the heart of downtown, we've dedicated ourselves to preserving the rich culture of vinyl by offering a carefully curated selection of records from classic legends to the newest indie artists. Whether you're a seasoned collector or a curious newcomer, our store is a welcoming space where music lovers of all kinds can explore, discover, and connect. Our passion for analog sound drives everything we do, and we’re proud to be part of a global community that keeps the spirit of vinyl alive.
