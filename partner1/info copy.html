Our History

Brandy Mellive was founded in Italy in the early 1980s by a father and son duo, Silvio and Stephan Marsan.

The brand was created to sell California-style attire.

The first store in the US was in 2009, in Los Angeles California. The name is inspired by a fictional story of an American girl named brandy and an English boy named Melville.

Why Brandy Melville?

Brandy Melville offers affordable clothing with a style that is always on trend.

Brandy Melville offers a one-size fits all approach to fashion.

Brandy Melville strives for convience, with products now located at other well known stores such as PacSun and Nordstrom.

We currently have 133 stores located worldwide, including the US, Canada, Europe and the recent expansion into Asia.
