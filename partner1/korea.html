Brandy Melville's Arrival in Korea

Brandy Melville, led by CEO Stephan Marsan, has officially opened its first store in Korea on January 3rd in Seongsu-dong, Seoul. From the opening day through the weekend, the store experienced long queues of eager customers.

Located at Seongsu-dong, Seoul, the Brandy Melville store is a three-story standalone building, offering a diverse selection of products.

Although it is currently the winter season, the store carries a variety of transitional and summer items, maintaining a roughly 50:50 balance between winter and summer collections.
