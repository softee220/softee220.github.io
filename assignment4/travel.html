Travel Spots During the Semester

New York City: It's about a 4.5 hour bus ride from Penn State. Since there’s so much to see — Times Square, museums, Broadway, Statue of Liberty etc — I recommend going as often as your weekend allows!

Tampa: This Florida city feels cleaner and more peaceful than Miami. If you love baseball, definitely catch a Tampa Bay Rays game. Don’t miss the ocean views at Clearwater Beach either!

Pittsburgh: A small city that’s perfect for a weekend trip. The Andy Warhol Museum and the incline ride to enjoy Pittsburgh’s night views from above are must-dos!

Spring Break

Cancun: During spring break, I recommend traveling outside the U.S., and Cancun, Mexico, is one of the best destinations! The ocean was honestly the most beautiful I’ve ever seen. You can enjoy the Selha Water Park, visit Chichen Itza (one of the Seven Wonders of the World), and stay in all-inclusive resorts. Especially if you're doing exchange in the spring semester, it's a perfect chance to escape the cold!
